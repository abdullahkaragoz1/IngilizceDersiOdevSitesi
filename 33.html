33. Choose a term to complete the sentences



    tracks   hard disk   access time   portable hard drive  
    sectors   back up   transfer rate   floppy disk drive  



1. The first rule of data storage is to make a __back up__ of all important files.

2. A __floppy disk drive__ is slower than a hard drive and can only hold 1.44 MB.

3. The __hard disk__ inside your PC is made of aluminium alloy covered with a
magnetic coating. This makes the disk itself a rigid plate.

4. __tracks__ are circles around a disk and the __sector__ are segments
within each circle.

5. This hard drive is a 60 GB IBM model with a fast __access time__ of 8 ms.

6. The __transfer rate__ is the rate of transmission of data from the disk to the CPU.
This is usually described in megabytes per second.

7. Apple's iPod music player can double as a __hard drive__ for transporting
computer data
