44. Match the words with the sentences



    divx   video   mpeg   audio   reality   graphics  
    extension   compress   animation   streaming   multimedia  



1. A series of still images shown in sequence. = __animation__

2. __audio__ files are processed by sound software.

3. In medicine, doctors use virtual __reality__ systems to simulate
particular situations.

4. The suffix placed after a dot at the end of a filename. = __extension__

5. A format used to compress and transmit movies over the Web. = __mpeg__

6. People use special programs to __compress__ and decompress files
so that they occupy less disk space.

7. A video format developed by the Moving Pictures Experts Group. = __divx__

8. A system of filming, processing and showing moving pictures. = __video__

9. .gif stands for __streaming__ interchange format.

10. The technique which allows you to play music and watch video
before the entire file has downloaded. = __graphics__

11. The combination of moving and still images, sound, music and words. = __multimedia__
